    ❓ Help & Commands Player AI – Your Personal Assistant

    💬 Chat & Messaging

    • Type your question in the input field and press Enter or click ↑ to send.
    • Player answers with text, voice, and a wave animation in the right panel.
    • Use the Speak button (🎤) in the left panel to talk – your speech will be converted to text and sent.
    • While Player is speaking, the send button becomes a ⏹️ stop button – click it to stop speech and cancel the response.

    📎 Attachment Menu (🔗 button next to send)

    • Attach Photo/Video – upload an image or video file.
    • Add File – upload any file (text, document, etc.).
    • Take Screenshot – captures your screen (select a window or entire screen).
    • Deep Thinking – activates extra reasoning; Player will reflect more thoroughly.
    • Deep Research – searches multiple academic and reliable sources (currently a placeholder).

    🖱️ Air Mouse (Hand Gesture Control)

    • Click the Air mouse button in the left panel.
    • Allow camera access. Your index finger will move the on‑screen cursor.
    • Pinch (move fingertip toward thumb) to click.
    • Press Escape or say “stop hand control” to exit.

    👤 Face & Body Profiles

    • Use the radar chart’s Looks or Physique sections, or say “open face profile”.
    • Scan your face/body with the camera (MediaPipe models).
    • You can also edit values manually in the modal.

    📋 To‑Do List & Goals & Schedule

    • Access via the radar chart or by saying “open todo”, “open goals”, “open schedule”.
    • Add, edit, delete tasks. Mark them as done.
    • Search tasks by title or description.
    • Goals are organised by timeframes (Day, Week, Month, Year1‑10).

    📊 Progress Rank & Radar Chart

    • The radar chart (right panel) shows your progress in five areas: Looks, Physique, Goal Setting, To‑Do List, Schedule.
    • Your overall rank (E to SSS) updates automatically.
    • Click any radar label to open the corresponding editor.

    🗣️ Voice Commands (try saying these)

    • “what can you do” – lists all capabilities.
    • “today’s news” – fetches top headlines (RSS + AI fallback).
    • “open youtube” / “go to gmail” – opens websites.
    • “take screenshot” – captures screen.
    • “open notepad” – opens built‑in notepad.
    • “type hello world” – copies text to clipboard.
    • “open folder” – grants access to a local folder, then you can “list files”, “read file ”, “search files for ”.
    • “open vscode” / “open spotify” – attempts to launch desktop apps.

    🖍️ Sidebar & Chat Management

    • New chat – starts a fresh conversation.
    • Search – filters your chat list by name.
    • Chats – opens the chat manager (rename, pin, archive, delete, reorder).
    • Right‑click (⋮) on a chat for more options.
    • Collapse the sidebar with the ☰ button.

    🔐 Login & Profile

    • Click Log in to use Google, Apple, phone, or email (demo – verification code appears in console).
    • After login, click your avatar to edit your profile (username, display name, email, phone, profile picture).
    • Logged‑in users’ data is saved permanently in browser storage.

    ⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

    • Enter – send message.
    • Arrow Down (when sidebar not collapsed) – hide chat list.
    • Arrow Right – show chat list.
    • Escape – stop hand control / close some menus.

    📖 Learn More – Player AI Guide Everything you can do with your assistant

    🚀 Getting Started

    Welcome message – When the page loads, Player says “Welcome back sir, what can I do for you?” – you can start typing or speaking immediately.

    Interface – Left panel: chat list and actions (New chat, Search, Chats, Air mouse, Speak, Login). Center: conversation. Right panel: radar chart (progress) and wave visualizer (audio reactive).

    💬 Chatting with Player

    • Type your message in the input field and press Enter or click the ➤ button.
    • Player answers with text, voice, and the wave visualizer pulses.
    • While Player speaks, the send button turns into a stop ⏹️ – click it to interrupt speech and cancel any pending response.
    • Use the 🗣️ Speak button (left panel) to talk – your speech is transcribed and sent automatically.

    📎 Attachment Menu (🔗 button)

    Click the 🔗 button next to the send button to open a menu with five tools:

    • 📸 Attach Photo/Video – upload an image or video file. Preview appears in chat.
    • 📄 Add File – upload any document (text, PDF, etc.).
    • 🖵 Take Screenshot – captures your screen (choose a window or entire screen).
    • 🧠 Deep Thinking – enables extra reasoning; Player will reflect more thoroughly before answering.
    • 🔬 Deep Research – searches multiple academic and reliable sources (currently a placeholder you can extend).

    🖱️ Air Mouse (Hand Gesture Control)

    • Click the Air mouse button in the left panel.
    • Allow camera access. Your index finger moves the cursor on screen.
    • Pinch (move fingertip toward thumb) to click.
    • Press Escape or say “stop hand control” to exit.

    👤 Face & Body Profiles

    • Open via the radar chart (click “Looks” or “Physique”) or say “open face profile” / “open body profile”.
    • Click Scan Face Now or Scan Body Now – the camera will take three samples and analyse your features.
    • You can also manually edit each value (e.g., face shape, skin tone, shoulder width).
    • The data is saved in your browser and contributes to your Progress Rank.

    📋 To‑Do List, Goals & Weekly Schedule

    To‑Do List – say “open todo” or click the radar label “To Do List”. Add tasks with title and description, mark them done, search, edit, delete.
    Goals – say “open goals”. Create goals with timeframes (Day, Week, Month, Year1…Year10). Each goal can have a description and creation date.
    Weekly Schedule – say “open schedule”. Add tasks for each day of the week. Optionally set a time.
    All contribute to your Progress Rank.

    📊 Progress Rank & Radar Chart

    The radar chart (right panel) shows your scores in five areas: Looks, Physique, Goal Setting, To‑Do List, Schedule.
    • Hover over any label to see a tooltip with details.
    • Click a label to open the corresponding editor.
    • Your overall rank (E → D → C → B → A → S → SS → SSS) updates automatically.
    • The wave visualizer animates when Player speaks.

    🗣️ Voice Commands (Try these)

    General
    • “what can you do” – lists all capabilities.
    • “today’s news” – fetches top headlines (tries BBC, CNN, Sky, then falls back to AI).
    • “take screenshot” – opens screen capture.
    • “open notepad” – opens the built‑in notepad (you can type, copy, download, clear).
    • “type hello world” – copies text to clipboard.
    • “open calculator” – opens Desmos scientific calculator in a new tab.
    • “open paint” – opens JSPaint (MS Paint web version).

    Web & Desktop Apps
    • “open youtube” / “go to gmail” / “visit reddit” – opens the website.
    • “search cats on youtube” – searches YouTube for “cats”.
    • “open vscode” – tries to launch VS Code (using vscode:// protocol).
    • “open spotify” – tries to launch Spotify.
    • “open folder” – grants access to a local directory (File System Access API). Then you can say “list files”, “read file ”, “search files for ”.

    Hand Control
    • “stop hand control” – stops the air mouse.

    🔐 Login & Profile

    • Click Log in → in the bottom‑left panel.
    • Choose Google, Apple, Phone, or enter an email address.
    • A 6‑digit verification code is shown in the browser console and an alert – enter it to complete login.
    • After login, click your avatar (bottom panel) to open the dropdown menu.
    • Select ☑ Profile to edit your username, display name, email, phone number, and upload a profile picture.
    • Logged‑in users’ data is stored permanently in localStorage.

    🖍️ Chat Management

    • New chat – starts a fresh conversation.
    • Search – filters chats by name (inline search bar).
    • Chats – opens the chat manager: you can rename, pin/unpin, archive, unarchive, delete, reorder (drag and drop), and bulk delete selected chats.
    • Each chat has a three‑dot menu (⋮) for quick actions.
    • Collapse the sidebar with the ☰ button.

    ⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

    • Enter – send message.
    • Arrow Down (when sidebar expanded) – hide the recent chats list.
    • Arrow Right – show hidden chat list.
    • Escape – stop hand control / close floating menus.

    📱 File System Access

    • Say “open folder” – select a folder on your computer (Chrome/Edge only).
    • Then you can say:
    – “list files” – shows all file names.
    – “read file ” – displays content of text files.
    – “search files for ” – finds files containing specific text (up to 500KB).
    – “directory info” – shows folder name and file count.

    🔧 Notepad & Clipboard

    • The built‑in notepad (say “open notepad”) lets you write notes, copy all, download as .txt, clear, and close. It automatically saves your content.
    • The clipboard helper copies text and shows a toast when you say “type ...” or “copy ...”.

    💡 Tip: Almost everything can be done by voice – just press the 🎤 Speak button and say what you want.
